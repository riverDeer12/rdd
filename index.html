It connects businesses, researchers, and public sectors to collaborate on research, development, and digital technologies, creating high-value products and services through a multidisciplinary approach utilizing regional resources and expertise.

Yufe

As part of The Young Universities for the Future of Europe (YUFE) network, this regional digital innovation platform fosters knowledge transfer between academia and the economy across the European Union. It connects businesses, entrepreneurs, researchers, and public sectors from different EU regions to collaborate on research, development, and digital technologies. By facilitating cross-border networking and innovation, the platform helps create high-value products and services through a multidisciplinary approach, utilizing resources, expertise, and human capital from across Europe.

Snow Festival

Website for the Snow Festival on Sljeme, Croatia, offers a comprehensive and user-friendly platform designed to enhance the visitor experience at this premier winter event. Featuring a detailed schedule of snow sports competitions, cultural performances, and family-friendly activities, the website provides easy access to ticket purchasing, event registration, and essential visitor information, including travel and accommodation options. It also highlights festival sponsors, partners, and local businesses, while promoting sustainability and community involvement.
