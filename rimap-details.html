Rimap

A regional digital innovation platform in Primorje-Gorski Kotar County promotes knowledge transfer between academia and the economy to strengthen the local innovation ecosystem. It connects businesses, researchers, and public sectors to collaborate on research, development, and digital technologies, creating high-value products and services through a multidisciplinary approach utilizing regional resources and expertise.
